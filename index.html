what is animation

Animation is the art of creating the illusion of movement by displaying a series of images in sequence. It is widely used in cartoons, movies, video games, and websites to make content more attractive and engaging. Each image, called a frame, is slightly different from the previous one. When these frames are shown quickly, they appear as smooth motion. Animation helps bring characters, objects, and ideas to life. It is used for entertainment, education, and advertising purposes. Modern animation can be created using both 2D and 3D computer software.

  1. Squash and Stretch
  2. Anticipation
  3. Staging
  4. Straight Ahead Action and Pose-to-Posev
  5. Follow Through and Overlapping Action
  6. Slow In and Slow Out
  7. Arcs
  8. Secondary Action
  9. Timing
  10. Exaggeration
  11. Solid Drawing
  12. Appeal
